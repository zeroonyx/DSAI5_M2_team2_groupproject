### **Business Report: Delivery Performance Optimization for Olist**

---

#### **1. Executive Summary**
Olist’s market competitiveness and customer retention are currently threatened by recurring delivery delays across its unified logistics network in Brazil. This report details the results of a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) aimed at identifying logistics bottlenecks. By isolating **seller processing lag** from **carrier transit days**, the project provides a data-driven foundation for establishing a concrete governance framework and enforcing carrier Service Level Agreements (SLAs).

#### **2. Problem Statement and Objectives**
The core operational challenge is Olist's inability to determine whether delays occur during the initial seller fulfillment phase or during carrier transit. To address this, the analytical team focused on:
*   **Transit days variance:** Measuring the time taken from carrier pickup to customer delivery.
*   **Seller processing lag:** Tracking the hours between order approval and handoff to the carrier.
*   **Regional late severity:** Identifying specific states where delivery delays are most acute.

#### **3. Analytical Methodology**
The insights were derived from a robust data pipeline using a **Medallion Architecture** and a **Kimball-style Star Schema**. Data was ingested via **Meltano**, transformed using **dbt**, and analyzed in **Jupyter Notebooks**. The analysis utilized the `order_delivered_carrier_date` as a critical marker to split the timeline between seller and carrier responsibilities.

#### **4. Key Findings from EDA (Data & Visualizations)**
The exploratory analysis revealed several critical trends regarding Olist’s fulfillment cycle based on visual evidence from the project's Jupyter Notebooks:

*   **Inconsistency in Delivery Times:** The **Distribution of Transit Days** histogram shows a wide spread of delivery durations, indicating significant variance in carrier performance across different routes.
*   **Impact on Customer Satisfaction:** The **Review Score vs. Delivery Performance** box plot demonstrates a direct link between delays and low ratings. Specifically, customers who gave a "1" or "2" review score experienced substantially higher median transit times compared to those who gave a "5".
*   **Negative Correlation Performance:** A **Regression Analysis** confirms a clear downward trend: as transit days increase, the average review score decreases linearly, highlighting the high cost of delivery delays on brand reputation.
*   **Regional Bottlenecks:** The **Late Severity Score per Region** heatmap identifies specific geographic clusters (likely states like SP, RJ, etc.) where delays are most severe. This allows Olist to target interventions in high-friction zones rather than applying a broad, less effective strategy.
*   **Metric Interdependence:** A **Correlation Matrix** identifies how various factors, such as seller processing time and carrier transit time, collectively contribute to the final delivery date and subsequent customer satisfaction.

#### **5. Strategic Recommendations**
Based on the data-driven insights, the following actions are recommended:
*   **Implement Carrier SLAs:** Use the transit days variance data to hold carriers accountable through strict performance-based contracts, particularly in high-severity regions identified in the heatmap.
*   **Seller Accountability Framework:** Identify and penalize bottleneck sellers who consistently exceed the "shipping limit date".
*   **Regional Logistics Optimization:** Focus operational improvements on the specific destination states where the **Late Severity Score** is highest.
*   **Automated Monitoring:** Move toward pipeline orchestration (e.g., via Airflow or GitHub Actions) to provide real-time updates on these KPIs.

#### **6. Conclusion**
By transitioning from raw data to a structured analytical warehouse, Olist now has the visibility required to isolate fulfillment failures. Addressing the specific points of failure visualized in the EDA—particularly the correlation between transit time and low review scores—will secure customer loyalty and operational continuity.
